A full quantitative equity research pipeline covering all 11 S&P 500 GICS sectors. The project extracts, cleans, and statistically analyses over 500 companies across income metrics, risk-adjusted returns, management efficiency, and growth indicators — culminating in an interactive Tableau dashboard for investment decision support.

Analytical Pipeline

Seven-stage treatment effects model applied to compare dividend yield distributions across all 11 S&P 500 GICS sectors.

STEP 01
Data Ingestion
500+ S&P 500 company CSVs loaded into MySQL in chunked batches via SQLAlchemy.
Python · pandas
STEP 02
Schema Design
60+ sector-partitioned tables for Income, Capital, Risk, Management, and Growth metrics.
MySQL · DDL
STEP 03
Normality Testing
Kruskal-Wallis non-parametric test on raw yields. Q-Q plot and histogram diagnostics confirm non-normality.
R · FSA
STEP 04
Variance Homogeneity
Levene's Test confirms heteroscedasticity (p < 0.05), mandating log1p transformation.
R · car
STEP 05
Log Transform
log1p applied to yields. Re-tested for variance homogeneity and improved normality profile.
R · base
STEP 06
Welch's ANOVA
Robust to unequal variances. Confirms at least one sector mean significantly differs (p < 0.05).
R · oneway.test
STEP 07
Games-Howell Post-Hoc
Pairwise comparisons valid under unequal variances and sample sizes. Identifies sector winners.
R · rstatix
STEP 08
Risk Profiling
Sharpe ratio thresholds classify sectors: Aggressive (>0.35), Balanced (0.25–0.35), Conservative (<0.25).
R · PerformanceAnalytics
